Porridge Beans

Description

This recipe shows how to make porridge beans.

This is a nice delicacy enjoyed in Nigeria

Ingredients

  • Beans
  • Palm Oil
  • Onion
  • Pepper
  • Crayfish
  • Salt
  • Seasoning
  • Water

Steps

  1. First boil the beans for 5-6 mins
  2. Sieve the beans
  3. Boil the beans again till it becomes soft
  4. Once the beans has boiled, Take it off the fire
  5. In a different pot, pour palm oil
  6. Cut your onions in the Palm oil and allow to fry
  7. Add your Seasonings, Pepper, Salt, Crayfish
  8. Add the boiled beans and mix it with the Palm Oil
  9. Add little Water and allow it to cook for 2-3 mins
