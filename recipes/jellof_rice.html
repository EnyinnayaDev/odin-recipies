Jellof Rice

Description

This is the recipefor Jellof Rice

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Tomatoe Paste
  • Onions
  • Pepper
  • Crayfish
  • Curry
  • Thyme
  • Seasoning Cube
  • Water

Steps

  1. Place a pot on the fire.
    Add water and perboil the rice
  2. After 5mins, take it off the fire and sieve the rice
  3. Then place the pot on the fire and eat it
  4. Pour some Oil in the pot
  5. Add Onions
  6. Add Tomatoe paste and stir it
  7. Allow it to fry for some time
  8. Add your Pepper, Seaoning cube, Curry and Thyme
  9. Add water and allow it to boil
  10. Add your rice and allow everything to boil
  11. When the rice has boiled bring it off the fire
