Yam and Egg Source

Description

A recipe for yam and egg source

Ingredients

  • Yam
  • Egg
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Seasoning Cube
  • Water

Steps

  1. Peel the Yam and cut it into big pieces
  2. Wash the Yam with water
  3. In a pot add Water, Salt and the Yam and place on the fire
  4. Allow the Yam to boil till it becomes soft
  5. Once the yam has boiled, take it off the fire
  6. Place a different pot on the fire
  7. Make sure the pot is properly dried
  8. Add the Vegetable Oil
  9. Cut the Tomatoe and Onion in the Oil and stir
  10. Allow it to fry for sometime
  11. Add Pepper, Seasoning Cube, and Salt
  12. Add a little Water
  13. Whisk your eggs add add them into the pot
  14. Allow the Eggs to fry a little and then stir it
  15. Allow to fry for 1-2mins, then bring it down from the fire
